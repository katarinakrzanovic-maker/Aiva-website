Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about AiVA
What is AiVA?+
AiVA is a personal AI assistant that helps with everyday tasks — writing emails, building CVs, translating text, helping with homework, suggesting recipes, and much more. It works in 6 languages and is available 24/7.
Do I need technical knowledge to use AiVA?+
No. AiVA is designed for everyone. Just type what you need in plain language — like you're texting a friend — and AiVA does the rest.
Is the free plan really free?+
Yes, completely free. No credit card required. The free plan gives you 10 AI tasks per day, access to all 6 languages, and basic tools. Upgrade to Basic for just $1/month for unlimited access.
Which languages does AiVA support?+
AiVA currently supports English, Serbian, Spanish, Arabic, Italian, and Chinese. More languages are being added based on user demand.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?+
Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no penalties. Cancel whenever you want directly from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
Is my data safe with AiVA?+
Yes. All communications are SSL encrypted. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your conversations remain private.
